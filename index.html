Margot Helen Tenenbaum is an American playwright. She is the adopted daughter of Royal and Etheline Tenenbaum. Her early reverence for Shakespeare, 19th century Russian dramatists, and French Existentialists led to her being awarded a Braverman Grant in the 9th grade. She is routinely praised for her ability to craft complex emotional experiences in both character and audience alike.
